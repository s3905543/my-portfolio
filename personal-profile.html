Personal Profile

Myer-Briggs Test

These results, explain some of my actions a lot better than I would be able to. The results say I am ‘Turbulent Logician’ which means that I am abstract thinking and very enthusiastic with new ideas. According to the results, I am also very private and withdrawn and can be very insensitive at times, both these aspects I do see in my everyday actions and my thoughts. After seeing these results, I definitely believe that I will watch my behaviour when working in teams. I can’t, as a logician, over correct someone and I cannot withdraw from a project.I will also watch who I pick if I do get to pick a team. As a turbulent logician, I would not pick another logician as they do not work well together as they want to work alone. I would want to work with an architect.



Learning Style Test

This result shows how I learn. It helps my understanding grow and how I can further develop and better my learning style. Also shows how I can better my learning in full. I think this result will make me watch my behaviour more when working in a group. I feel like I will listen more as I do seem to learn more from listening. If I catch myself humming to myself or bouncing my knee, I’ll know to refocus on the task in front of me. If I was making a team, I’d try to get more people who tactile learners and people who are more inclined to talk what they have learnt to better myself.



Big Five Personality Test

These results confer with the 2 previous tests. They agree that I am a private, withdrawn person. These results really hammers the nail in the coffin. They show that I am in the lower percentile for conscientiousness and intellect/imagination. They also show that I am high-scoring for agreeableness. All of these result indicate that yes I am a turbulent logician, someone who is happy to agree to stop any conflict and that I am very distant with peers. This gives me something to work on. I do not want to just agree to stop any chance of conflict. I will try and push my ideas more forward. With making groups, I would not get more people who are like me but opposites. So we can move forward with ideas, that are good. If I have a group that are full of turbulent logicians, we would all agree and potentially put forth a bad project/assignment because we were too scared of conflict.
